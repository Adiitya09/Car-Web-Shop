KIA Moters in India

Kia India is a subsidiary of the Korean car manufacturer Kia Motors, established in India with a dedicated manufacturing plant in Anantapur district, Andhra Pradesh, where they produce cars like the Seltos, Sonet, Carnival, and Carens; Kia entered the Indian market in 2019 with a focus on producing "Made for India" vehicles, boasting a large production capacity of around 300,000 units annually, and has rapidly gained market share through its modern designs and competitive pricing.
