Volkswagen Motors in India

Volkswagen India is a subsidiary of the German automotive manufacturer Volkswagen Group. It was established in 2007 and is headquartered in Pune, Maharashtra. Volkswagen India manufactures and sells cars from the Volkswagen Group, including Volkswagen, Audi, Škoda, Porsche, and Lamborghini.
