Electric cars in India

Electric vehicles (EVs) are automobiles powered by one or more electric motors, using electricity as their primary source of energy. They operate without a traditional internal combustion engine (ICE) that relies on fossil fuels. Instead, EVs use rechargeable batteries to store and provide electricity for propulsion.In India there are 15 to 20 electric cars
